Why this App?

Principles of Economics is a glossary mobile app of economic terms, with animated videos and interactive exercises that support the learning of economics.

The app covers the microeconomics topics in the course of AF3625 Engineering Economics (The Hong Kong Polytechnic University). It is designed to help you to:
  1. Learn economics at anytime and anywhere

  2. Get familiar with economics concepts

  3. Prepare for lectures

  4. Test and strengthen your understanding of concepts covered in lectures
